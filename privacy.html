Privacy

RoleMap AI is a static website. It does not send your data to a server.

  • Inputs you type (resume text, job description) stay in your browser.
  • If you click “Save Inputs”, the data is saved in your browser’s local storage only.
  • When you copy the prompt and paste into ChatGPT, your data is governed by ChatGPT’s policies.

Recommendation: Do not paste highly sensitive personal identifiers. You can remove phone/address if needed.
