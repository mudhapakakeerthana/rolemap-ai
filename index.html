RoleMap AI

A career positioning copilot for students and early-career professionals. It generates a structured strategy you can paste into ChatGPT: best-fit roles, readiness scores, narrative gaps, and a 90-day roadmap.
How it works
  1. Fill the fields below
  2. Click Generate & Copy Prompt
  3. Paste into ChatGPT
Tip: This is designed to reduce random applications by producing a role-focused plan.
Problem
Many applicants optimize resumes but lack a clear positioning strategy: which roles fit, what gaps matter most, and how to pitch their story.
Why GenAI
GenAI can synthesize background + goals + constraints into a structured strategy (not just keyword matching). This tool enforces a consistent, grounded output format.
Prompt Builder
Fill only what you have. The tool will generate one “master prompt” you can run in ChatGPT.
